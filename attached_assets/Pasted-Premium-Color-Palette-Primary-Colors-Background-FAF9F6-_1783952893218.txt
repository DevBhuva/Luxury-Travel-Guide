Overall Design Prompt

Create an ultra-premium luxury travel website inspired by Apple's minimalist design philosophy and the elegance of world-class luxury hospitality brands. Use a refined Gold and White color palette with ivory backgrounds, champagne gold accents, soft glassmorphism, large rounded corners (28–40px), generous whitespace, cinematic full-screen travel imagery, editorial typography, floating cards, pill-shaped buttons, subtle shadows, and smooth motion. Avoid clutter, bright colors, and heavy gradients. Every page should feel calm, sophisticated, and exclusive, like a private luxury travel concierge rather than a standard travel agency. Use gold only for key accents, CTAs, icons, and highlights so the interface remains clean, timeless, and luxurious. The final experience should be worthy of an Awwwards-winning website and leave visitors with an immediate impression of trust, elegance, and aspiration.
